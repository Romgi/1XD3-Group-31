Good Web Design

Adobe Business Website

Consistency - The site keeps a stable visual system across pages, including layout, typography, and navigation.

Ease of Navigation - The navigation stays consistent, which makes it easy to move between sections without getting lost.

Universal Usability and Accessibility - The content is readable, responsive, and built with clear contrast and predictable structure.

Error Prevention - The interface is polished and avoids obvious broken flows in normal use.

Bad Web Design

Berkshire Hathaway Website

Consistency - The site has little visual structure and weak page-to-page consistency.

Ease of Navigation - Users have to read long lists of plain links with almost no visual guidance.

Universal Usability and Accessibility - Small text, weak visual hierarchy, and poor responsiveness make the experience harder to use.

Error Prevention - The site avoids complex interaction, but that simplicity also leaves little support for users who need guidance.
