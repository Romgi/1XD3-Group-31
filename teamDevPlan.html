The group we are designing the website for is McMaster Concert Band and the name of the app is ConcertHelper.

The Purpose

The purpose of this app is to provide both a public-facing information hub and an internal platform for music distribution and band resources for the McMaster Concert Band. We have chosen to build this particular product because the McMaster Concert Band faces two big problems: current band members do not have easy online access to their music parts, reference recordings, and season information, and concertgoers do not have easy access to information about the ensemble, upcoming concerts, past performances, and recordings. The goal of our website is to provide ease of access to relevant information for concertgoers and current band members.

Data Description

The first database table will store the reference recordings for band members sorted by part in the ensemble. We need a database to store all reference recordings for each individual part. This information will be for band members.

  • recording_id(VARCHAR) - PK
  • part_name(VARCHAR)
  • recording_url(VARCHAR)

The second database table will store the music parts for each instrument based on the current concert being played. We need this table since it will give us the sheet music based on the current concert and part for the band member. This information will be for band members.

  • part_id(VARCHAR) - PK
  • instrument_part(VARCHAR)
  • pdf_url(VARCHAR)

The third database table will store past performances of the concert band. It will store URLs to MP4s or uploaded YouTube links to past performances. This information will be for band members.

  • concert_id(VARCHAR) - PK
  • concert_url(VARCHAR)

The fourth database table will store information about each member of the ensemble and will include a picture and a basic description. This information will be public.

  • member_id(VARCHAR) - PK
  • photo_url(VARCHAR)
  • description(TEXT)

The fifth database table will store information about any upcoming concerts for the general public.

  • concert_id(VARCHAR) - PK
  • description(TEXT)

Functions Performed By App

Admin: As an admin you have functionality of a band user and a general user, but you get access to a control panel where you can add remove and update reference recordings, music parts, past performances, ensemble information, and upcoming concerts. For each of the different things you can modify, there will be a panel with a dropdown menu showing a list of all recordings or music parts and from there you can update urls and add or remove any text or pdfs.

  • Add, remove, and update reference recordings (INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE)
  • Add, remove, and update music parts (INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE)
  • Add, remove, and update past performances (INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE)
  • Add, remove, and update ensemble information (INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE)
  • Add, remove, and update upcoming concerts (INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE)
Band User: As a band user you have the functionality of a general user in addition to access to a band member panel. As a band user, you will be able to see your current music parts associated with your account as well as a list of reference recordings for your instrument.
  • View reference recordings (SELECT)
  • View music parts (SELECT)
General User: As a general user, you have access to a panel which contains a scrollable menu that have links to past performances and you have access to a bulletin board that shows the dates of any upcoming concerts
  • View past performances (SELECT)
  • View upcoming concerts (SELECT)

Client Feedback

Some feedback they gave us was they wanted to make it less McMaster themed and more "their own brand". They gave us some different ways of doing this like giving us their logo which we will implement as well as tone down a lot of McMaster colour scheme in replacement for their own. We changed our plan from a mainly McMaster themed webpage to one that suits their style much more.
